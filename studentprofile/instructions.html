Cognizant - Aptitude Round
120 Minutes

Instructions

Check System Requirements: Ensure your device meets the technical requirements (browser, internet speed, etc.) and test your webcam and microphone beforehand. It is suggested to use Chrome as a browser.

Stable Internet: Use a reliable internet connection to avoid interruptions.

Quiet Environment: Take the exam in a quiet, well-lit space with no distractions or background noise.

Follow Proctoring Guidelines: Stay within the camera frame at all times, and avoid leaving the exam screen.

No External Devices: Do not use mobile phones, calculators, or other devices unless explicitly allowed.

No Assistance: Ensure you do not receive help from others or use unauthorized resources during the exam.

Maintain Focus: Avoid looking away from the screen repeatedly, as eye-tracking and face detection may flag suspicious behavior.

Follow Time Limits: Keep an eye on the time and manage it wisely.

Submit Before Time: Submit your answers before the deadline to ensure they are recorded.
